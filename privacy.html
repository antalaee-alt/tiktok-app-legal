Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you use the TikTok Auto Poster application ("the App"), we request access to your TikTok account via OAuth. The information we access is limited to the scopes you approve, specifically:

  • Basic profile information (to verify your identity).
  • Permissions to upload and publish videos on your behalf.

2. How We Use Your Information

The App uses your authentication tokens solely for the purpose of executing the actions you initiate (e.g., posting a generated video to your TikTok account). We do not use your data for any other purpose.

3. Data Storage

As a development application, we do not permanently store your personal data, TikTok videos, or authentication tokens on our servers. Tokens are used temporarily during your active session.

4. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer to outside parties your personally identifiable information or TikTok data.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer.
